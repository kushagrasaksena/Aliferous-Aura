Can You Listen by Chhavi

The Raven Reads

The earth whispers to you
be a hurricane
Dissolve yourself
in your self created chaos
And then
Brush the dirt of your sleeve

The earth whispers to you
be a forest fire
let the hurt bleed
let the fire heat
let it spread
burn yourself up
and then
rise back from the ashes like a phoenix.

The earth whispers to you
be a wallflower
be pretty enough
to give the world It's smile back
be wild enough
to let them think tem times
before they pluck you off and drop you down
Oh, and it they do then you bury yourself deep for a moment
And then
you grow
